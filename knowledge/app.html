The Best App for Teachers To Track Student Progress

Cutting-edge software designed to track student progress for college and university teachers

Formative assessment saves time
for auto-grading

Our app for teachers to track student progress allows you to automatically assign grading weight to participation, as well as interactive questions and homework to be completed beyond the classroom activities. You can then upload the results from each class directly into the Top Hat gradebook. All responses and participation grades are captured instantly in the Top Hat gradebook for students to access any time, and educators to analyze, and keep track of student achievement, or areas of difficulty throughout the learning process. Integration with your learning management system is smooth, and our educational apps also help you reduce your admin overhead—you can analyze auto-graded results to gauge comprehension and identify concepts to review or reinforce in your next lecture or for your subsequent lessons plans. And you can export grades easily to your learning management system.

Student progress tracking that verifies whether students are present

Top Hat’s apps for teachers to track student progress also allow instructors to accurately verify whether a student is physically present in class… and stays present. An educator can easily take attendance by prompting students to sign in on their devices using a unique code, and auto-assign grades for attendance. Top Hat uses proprietary market- leading technology—a combination of GPS and Bluetooth— to verify the physical location of students, ensuring that only those students attending the instruction get accurately counted. For full flexibility, there is extra functionality to excuse absences during the school year.

Eliminate the need for extra
hardware

Unlike older education technology such as traditional clickers, Top Hat’s 21st-century active learning software syncs directly with the devices students already own and use, such as tablets including iPads, phones and laptops. It works on multiple devices. There’s no need for students to purchase additional hardware. Simply download our app for teachers to track student progress and away you go. We also integrate with popular learning management systems, including Moodle, Blackboard and Desire2Learn.

High stakes testing in a secure
environment

For summative assessment and final exams, the Top Hat Test app allows you to assess your students in a secure browser that automatically detects cheating. Students can only access the test by inputting a unique code that the instructor auto-generates and shares in class. Accountability is key: proprietary algorithms identify when students are cheating and lock them out of the test automatically, and it’s up to the instructor to decide whether they can be let back in based on browser activity. Top Hat Test is compatible with any student device with no need for any separate downloads.

Make learning more collaborative and increase participation

Top Hat’s apps for teachers to track student progress allow professors to run real-time discussions, embedded within the flow of a lecture. Students can choose to participate anonymously, ensuring that everyone feels comfortable sharing their thoughts, even when the subject matter is sensitive. Instructors can also use Top Hat to encourage and facilitate group work and peer-to-peer collaboration and interaction for assignments.

In the past few years, there’s been a spike in technological learning strategies in higher education. Things like online learning, blended learning, elearning, MOOCs and distance learning have become increasingly popular. As instructors move towards newer classroom assessment techniques, the need for a reliable technology platform becomes more important. That’s where Top Hat Classroom shines. Our software uses smartphones, laptops, and tablets to facilitate engagement, allowing any educator to create a dynamic classroom environment than can rapidly track student achievement.
