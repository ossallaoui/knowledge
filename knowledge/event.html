National Day of Reading

The role of the public library in the cultural animation system

The Ministry of Culture and Communication organized on Thursday in Rabat, the direction of the book, libraries and archives, a meeting around the theme "The place of the public library in the system of cultural animation", on the occasion of the celebration on May 10th of the National Day of Reading. This meeting was devoted to the debate on the issue of the concept of cultural animation in the public library, its forms and its objectives, as well as the contribution of the evaluation of public libraries to the improvement of their services, indicates a statement from the Ministry of Culture and Communication.

The meeting is part of the implementation of the practical and executive plan of the culture department of the Ministry of Culture and Communication in the field of reading and writing, aiming at the implementation of several related pillars. evaluating the organizational and contractual arrangements for book support, and increasing the department's resources in support of publication. It is also about setting up mechanisms to support efforts to encourage the publishing, publishing and public reading industry, through the implementation of measures to promote public reading. ,
